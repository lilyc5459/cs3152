PATHOGENSIS ALPHA play instructions:

WASD to move
Hold SPACE to convert, conversion range is displayed as a red circle

To win, defeat the boss by infecting it (there are two of them right now, there will only be one per level). The boss in this level is the big two headed thing.

Health displayed at top left
Conversion power displayed below health bar, recharges over time

Collect yellow items to increase health
Collect blue items to increase conversion power

Enemies and items spawn randomly (100 enemies max) - this will all change later with level design, spawn points, regions, etc

Blue enemies are UNCONVERTABLE. (Will change to have slightly different art later)

The pulsating organs are infection points that will drop items when infected.
